Request Hosting
---------------
Submitted by Lance Albertson on April 5, 2013
Please give us a few days or a week to discuss and respond to your
request. This form will send an email to our support ticket system.
Name * Email * Project Name * Project URL * Software License * What
software license does your project primarily use? Estimated size of
user community * Other pertinent information about your project? The
type(s) of server(s) or service(s) you are requesting hosting for, if
applicable. * The purpose of said server or service (will it be Web,
development, etc?). * Your estimated bandwidth usage. * Contributions
that you may be able to make to cover costs. * We realize that most
projects are not in a position to help cover the costs, but any
contribution certainly helps!
